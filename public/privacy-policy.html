This Privacy Policy describes how ASH Protocol ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the ASH COIN mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

01 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated automatically through your use of the App.

DATA TYPE EXAMPLES PURPOSE
Account Data Email, display name, profile photo URL Account creation and authentication
Mining Data Mining sessions, hashrate, ASH balance, boost activity Core app functionality
Device Data Device type, OS version, unique device identifiers App performance and security
Usage Data Features used, session duration, click events App improvement and analytics
Referral Data Referral codes, referred users Syndicate/team features
Transaction Data Withdrawals, purchases, ledger entries Financial record keeping

02 How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features
  • Process mining sessions and calculate ASH token rewards
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Send notifications about mining completion, boosts, and daily rewards
  • Process referral commissions and syndicate rewards
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Send administrative messages about your account

03 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and storage services
  • Advertising Partners: Google AdMob for serving advertisements within the App
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: With your explicit consent for any other purpose

⚠️ We never sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

04 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit.

  • All data transmissions use TLS/SSL encryption
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict access to your data only to your account
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time

Your ASH balance and mining history are stored on our Firebase database. While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your account.

05 Third-Party Services

The App integrates third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, Firestore database, and cloud storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — Optional OAuth login. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — In-app advertising to support free features. AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Expo / React Native — Mobile app framework for push notifications

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services.

06 Advertising

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob to support our free mining features. By using the App:

  • AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID (GAID/IDFA)
  • Ads may be personalized based on your interests and browsing behavior
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • Watching rewarded ads is optional — it activates mining boosts
  • Banner ads are displayed at the bottom of the main screen

To opt out of personalized ads on Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization. On iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track".

07 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.
